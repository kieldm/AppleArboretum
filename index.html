Thukral and Tagra are a Delhi-based artist duo comprising of Jiten Thukral and Sumir Tagra. Driven by the artistic methodologies of painting, gaming, archiving, and publishing, their multifaceted studio practice reflects the scope of engagement in the cultural and political landscape of India and the world.

Kiel M.
Kiel Mutschelknaus is a motion and generative designer based in the DC-Baltimore area. Kiel's studio focuses on crafting generative tools to create bespoke typography, image, and motion work. His coding project, SpaceTypeGenerator.com, is an open source tool that allows users to create their own kinetic type experiments.
